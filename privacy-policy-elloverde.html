01

Who We Are

Elloverde Landscaping & Flora is a company specialized in landscaping, gardening and the commercialization of ornamental plants, located in Fortaleza, Ceará. Our commitment goes beyond greenery: we value the trust you place in us when sharing your personal data.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store and protect the personal information of visitors and clients of our website and services.

02

Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

2.1 Data provided by you

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number or WhatsApp
  • Delivery or service address (for landscaping projects)
  • Messages sent through the contact form or email
  • Payment information (when applicable, processed by secure platforms)

2.2 Automatically collected data

  • IP address and device type
  • Browser and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Access source (links, social media, searches)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 07)

03

How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your quote requests, inquiries and customer service
  • Processing orders and managing deliveries of products and services
  • Sending communications about our services, promotions and news (with consent)
  • Improving your browsing experience on our website
  • Analyzing website performance and usage for continuous improvement
  • Complying with applicable legal and regulatory obligations
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring information security

04

Legal Basis for Processing

In accordance with Brazil's General Data Protection Law (Law No. 13,709/2018 — LGPD), the processing of your personal data is carried out based on the following legal grounds:

Consent

When you expressly authorize the use of your data (e.g., newsletter sign-up)

Contract performance

When necessary for the provision of contracted services

Legitimate interest

For website improvements, security and institutional communications

Legal obligation

When required by law or competent authority

05

Data Sharing

Elloverde does not sell, rent or trade your personal data. We may share it only in the following situations:

  • With logistics and delivery partners, for order fulfillment
  • With payment platforms, to process transactions securely
  • With digital marketing tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Meta Ads), anonymized whenever possible
  • With public authorities, when required by law or court order

In all cases, we require third parties to respect the confidentiality and security of your data.

06

Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secure environment, with access restricted to authorized staff. We adopt appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, alteration or improper disclosure.

Data will be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by legal obligations. After that period, it will be securely deleted.

07

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies: required for the basic functioning of the website
  • Analytical cookies: to understand how visitors use the site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Marketing cookies: to display relevant ads on other platforms

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

08

Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the LGPD, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Confirmation and access: to know whether we process your data and obtain a copy
  • Correction: to request updating of incomplete, inaccurate or outdated data
  • Anonymization, blocking or deletion: of unnecessary or non-compliant data
  • Portability: to receive your data in a structured, interoperable format
  • Deletion: to request erasure of data processed based on your consent
  • Withdrawal of consent: at any time, without prejudice to prior processing activities
  • Objection: to contest processing carried out on the basis of legitimate interest

To exercise your rights, please contact us through the channels listed in Section 10.

09

Links to Third-Party Sites

Our website may contain links to external pages, such as social media or partner websites. Elloverde is not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend that you read the privacy policy of each platform you visit.
